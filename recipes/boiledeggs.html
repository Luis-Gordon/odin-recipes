Boiled Eggs

This a recipe for preparing the perfect golden, runny, but firm hard boiled egg.

Place one egg in a pot and fill it with enough water to completely submerge it, while leaving about 1-2 cm between the egg and the surface of the water, then remove the egg.

Bring the water to a boil, then reduce the heat to about two thirds of maximum to avoid splashing and unnecessary rattling of the eggs. Gently place the eggs into the pot with a spoon and set a timer for between 5-7 minutes depending on your preference.

After the timer has ended remove the eggs again with a spoon, dry them off, and place them in egg cups, as they're now ready to be served. Enjoy.
